Vacancy description

A free and simple tool to track and analyze views of your GitHub profile. Profile view counter is an easy-to-use tool that allows you to quickly and accurately track the number of views on your GitHub profile.

You can use the profile views counter to analyze the dynamics of views of your profile, such as the impact of any development, blogging or participation in a conference. Also, you can use the profile views counter to see the statistics of referral registrations from your profile and their effect on the number of profile views.

Also, an important element of our activity is social responsibility, and we plan to direct profits to support the Armed Forces of Ukraine by checking receipts at check.gov.ua.
