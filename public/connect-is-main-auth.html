Tool for finding direct job contracts
Unlock Global Career Opportunities

Build your list of target companies, connect with Ukrainian professionals already working there, and take the first step toward your future job.

Why use it
Firstly, finding a job in foreign companies where Ukrainians are already employed is generally easier.
Secondly, your passive job search should enhance your future active job search.
Even if you’re happy with your job, still build your list of companies using the tool to establish contacts with professionals and improve your network.
How to use it
01
Explore Job Openings
In companies based in the USA, UK, and EU.
02
Find Company Profiles
Visit the LinkedIn profiles of these companies and copy their profile links.
03
Add to Your List
Paste the copied links into the tool to build your list of target companies.
04
Discover Professionals
The tool will generate links to the LinkedIn profiles of Ukrainian professionals already working in these companies.
05
Connect and Network
Reach out to these professionals to establish connections and potentially secure a recommendation for future job opportunities.
