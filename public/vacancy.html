Vacancy description

We are seeking a talented Middle UI/UX Designer with a minimum of 2 years of experience to join our creative team. We need a designer who can create design ideas, make our design system better, have an impact on product metrics, make decisions on their own, and stay in touch with the team. You will work on designing and enhancing our digital products to provide an outstanding user experience.

What we offer

  • Working with as minimum bureaucracy as possible in small dedicated teams.
  • Competitive salary and performance-based bonuses.
  • Flexible working hours and remote work options.
  • 20 paid vacation days per year and undocumented sick leaves.

Key Responsibilities

  1. Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including product managers, developers, and other designers, to create user-friendly interfaces and experiences.
  2. Conduct user research to understand user needs, behaviors, and pain points.
  3. Develop wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs.
  4. Ensure design consistency and adherence to brand guidelines.
  5. Stay up-to-date with the latest design trends and tools.

Team description

Our team is small, close-knit and works well together. Designers and developers work together to create a creative and dynamic environment.

First year work description

  • Collaborate closely with our senior designers and developers to gain hands-on experience in our design and development processes.
  • Work on various design projects, contributing to wireframing, prototyping, and creating high-fidelity designs.
  • Gain insights into our user base and help conduct user research to better understand their needs and preferences.
  • Be part of our agile development environment, attending daily stand-up meetings and contributing to the iterative design and development process.
  • Take on increasing responsibilities as you become more comfortable with our team's dynamics and projects.

If you are a UI/UX Designer with a strong portfolio and meet our requirements, join our team and contribute to creating exceptional user experiences for our products.
